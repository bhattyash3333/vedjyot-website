Meet Your Priest

Pandit Yash Bhatt

Pandit Yash Bhatt is dedicated to preserving the sacred traditions of Sanatan Dharma through authentic Vedic rituals and spiritual guidance. Having performed more than 250 pujas and Vedic ceremonies across Canada, he has earned the trust of families through his devotion, professionalism, and commitment to traditional Hindu scriptures. Fluent in English, Hindi, and Gujarati, he ensures that every ceremony is performed with precision while clearly explaining the significance of each ritual.
